SUMMARY OF THE PRODUCT

TechTrust Enterprise, Jan 15, 2022

Today’s world is more advanced and the current trends we use as our daily routines nowadays are mostly technology-related. In today’s world, everything is at your fingertips. So, the researchers have chosen to create a product that is branded as P2C USB.P2C USB is a magic combination of various applications, including voice to text, esignature, wireless presenter and computer, laptop, PC or remote control. It transforms your smartphone into a keyboard, mouse, laser pointer and writing pad. To spread its wings in the field of this product business, researchers have decided to build a company called as TechTrust Enterprise for the product to be operated. In this business plan, there are a few main plans which are administration plan, marketing plan, operational plan and financial plan. Every plan has its own budget allocated. Through administration plan will be elaborated on the administration section which includes organization chart, list of administrative personnel, schedule of tasks and responsibilities, schedule of remuneration, list of office equipment and supplies and administration budget. Other than that, a marketing plan describes an organization's advertising strategy for generating leads and reaching its target market. A marketing strategy outlines the outreach and programmes that will be carried out over time, as well as how the organisation will assess the impact of these initiatives. The points that will be explained in the marketing plan are profile of product & illustration, target market, sale forecast, marketing strategy and marketing budget. In addition, the operational plan highlights all of the daily operations and process planning that is essential to running a successful firm. The things to be discussed are such as process flowchart, material requirement, list of machines and equipment, layout plan of operation area/factory, operation overheads and operation budget. Lastly, a financial plan is a statement that details a company's current financial condition and long-term financial objectives, as well as strategies for achieving those objectives. The data that to be put together are project implementation cost, source of financing, profit and lost proforma and is the proposed profitable.

The company's name is TechTrust Enterprise. Instead, the product brand for the business is P2C USB. P2C stands for phone to computer. The ideas that led to the creation of P2C USB came from owners who were inspired by mobile phones and computer gadgets like mice and so on. The idea arose because of the complexity of the person having to carry various equipment in the meeting place and so on. As we know, in this day and age, every individual of any age is very concerned about mobile phones. So, the owners have figured out a way to please someone not to miss any things while going to a meeting or teachers who need to teach online or so don’t have to bother carrying a lot of stuff. P2C USB can transform your smartphone into a wireless presenter and voice input and esignature get your work done faster.
