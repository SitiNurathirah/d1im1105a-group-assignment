Product

Description

Features

1. P2C USB can transform your smartphone into a wireless mouse and presenter.

2. Voice Note - P2C USB can input texts in Word, PowerPoint, Outlook or browsers at up to 300 words per minute when you talking to your phone. Up to 100+ language it can recognise.

3. E-Signature - sign on your smartphone and transfer the signature immediately to any e-documents on computer or other gadget.

4. Wireless/remotely presenter- just the USB into your chosen computer and you can present what on your computer through your phone near or far.

5. Remote control-can be used to control your computer easily.
